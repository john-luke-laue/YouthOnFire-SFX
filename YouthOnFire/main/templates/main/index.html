Works of Charity

Our focus is serving the poor, needy, lonely, homeless, and vulnerable of Los Angeles. While we realize the importance and value of prayer and catechism, our mission is centered around service and charity. Ubi caritas et amor, Deus ibi est.

Serve at Your Own Pace

We realize people want to help but aren't always available to commit lots of time. So, we don't force you to make long-term commitments or attend countless meetings. We'll send out event information via our website, email, and social media platforms; all you have to do is show up and be ready to serve.

Inclusive Community

We accept all who have a willing heart. We are a Catholic based community here at Saint Francis Xavier Burbank, but we welcome all faiths and people of no faith. We are primarily oriented toward youth and young adults, but we welcome anyone who wants to help. So, please invite all your friends!
